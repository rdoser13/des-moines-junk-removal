# Des Moines Junk Removal Brand Guide

## Brand Direction

**Brand style:** Local, professional, dependable, and strong  
**Visual theme:** Metro hauling service with Des Moines identity  
**Core feel:** Clean, trustworthy, established, and easy to recognize

The logo uses a bold shield emblem with a junk removal truck, raised green dumpster, Des Moines skyline, and bridge detail. This gives the brand a strong local service feel while still looking polished enough for a modern website, Google Business Profile, truck wraps, yard signs, invoices, uniforms, and ads.

---

## Brand Personality

Des Moines Junk Removal should feel:

- **Reliable:** Customers should instantly feel like the job will get done right.
- **Local:** The skyline and bridge elements tie the company to the Des Moines metro.
- **Professional:** The badge shape, strong typography, and clean layout avoid a cheap “junk hauling” look.
- **Approachable:** The green accent keeps the brand from feeling too industrial or cold.
- **Established:** The shield style supports the “family-owned” and “12 years in business” messaging.

---

This will work better for:

- Website navigation

- Quote forms
- Top-left website branding
- Mobile header

### Icon/Favicon

Use the shield without the large text, or a simplified truck + green dumpster icon.

Best for:

- Favicon
- Social media profile image
- App-style icons
- Small footer logo
- Website loading graphics

## Color Palette

### Primary Navy

**Hex:** `#0D1B2A`  
**Use for:** Logo outlines, headers, navigation, buttons, footer, strong text

This is the main trust color. It gives the brand strength and makes it feel established.

### Junk Removal Green

**Hex:** `#4CAF50`  
**Use for:** CTA buttons, accent lines, icons, hover states, trust badges

This green gives the brand energy and makes it feel cleaner and more modern. It also subtly suggests responsible disposal, cleanup, and fresh spaces.

### Steel Gray

**Hex:** `#6B7280`  
**Use for:** Body text, secondary icons, skyline details, supporting sections

This helps soften the heavier navy and makes the website easier to read.

### Light Gray

**Hex:** `#E5E7EB`  
**Use for:** Section backgrounds, dividers, cards, form borders

This is useful for building clean page sections without making the site feel crowded.

### Off-White

**Hex:** `#F8FAFC`  
**Use for:** Website background, hero background, service cards

This will feel cleaner than pure white while still keeping the site bright.

### Pure White

**Hex:** `#FFFFFF`  
**Use for:** Logo background, cards, buttons on dark sections, form fields

## Typography

### Primary Headline Font

**Recommended font:** League Spartan  
**Use for:** H1, H2, large CTA text, service page headlines

Why it works: It matches the bold, industrial feel of the logo while staying clean and modern.

Recommended weights:

- H1: League Spartan Bold / 700
- H2: League Spartan Bold / 700
- H3: League Spartan SemiBold / 600

### Secondary Font

**Recommended font:** Montserrat  
**Use for:** Navigation, buttons, subheadings, service cards, labels

Why it works: It is clean, modern, and easy to read. It pairs well with a bold logo without competing with it.

Recommended weights:

- Navigation: Montserrat SemiBold / 600
- Buttons: Montserrat Bold / 700
- Small labels: Montserrat Medium / 500

### Body Font

**Recommended font:** Inter  
**Use for:** Paragraphs, FAQs, service descriptions, form text, footer copy

Why it works: Inter is very readable on websites and works well for local service businesses.

Recommended weights:

- Body copy: Inter Regular / 400
- Body bold: Inter SemiBold / 600

## Final Website Design Direction

The brand should look like a real, established Des Moines hauling company, not a random side hustle.
